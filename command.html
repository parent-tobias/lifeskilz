Command Principles

  • Knowing who you are
  • Knowing and Understanding Authority
  • Knowing there is No Gain Without Pain
  • Understanding Stewardship
  • Taking Responsibility
  • Domination of Your Environment
  • Understanding the Power of Freedom
  • The Five W's, Five W's and one H

1. Knowing who you are

Understanding the specific purposes for which God created each person, object, and relationship in my life and living in harmony with them.

2. Knowing and understand Authority

Honoring the responsibilities of parents, church leaders, government, and other authorities and learning how God works through them to provide direction and protection.

4. Knowing there is no gain without pain

Allowing the hurts from offenders to reveal “blind spots” in my own life, and then seeing how I can benefit their lives.

5. Understanding stewardship

Understanding that everything I have has been entrusted to me by God, and wisely using it for His purposes.

3. Taking responsibility

Realizing I am accountable to God for every thought, word, action, and motive.

6. Domination of your environment.

Discovering God’s purpose for my life by engrafting Scripture in my heart and mind, and using it to “think God’s thoughts” and make wise decisions.

7. Understanding the power of freedom

Enjoying the desire and power to do what is right, rather than claiming the privilege to do what I want.

8. The Five W's, Five W's and One H

Who, what, when, where, why and how are you really? How do you measure in the following areas of Life Skilz?

The Five Ws, Five Ws and one H, are questions whose answers are considered basic in information-gathering. They are often mentioned in journalism, research, and police investigations. They constitute a formula for getting the complete story on a subject. According to the principle of the Five Ws, a report can only be considered complete if it answers these questions starting with an interrogative word.

Can you give a good report on yourself based on the formula referenced above on the life skilz listed below?

Personal care
Do you like you enough to take the very best care of you?
Organization
Is your life in order/disorder
Respect for self and others
If you don’t love and cherish yourself you will never do the same for others
Communication
Do you understand my verbal and nonverbal or do I send a conflicting message? Do I have the ability to understand yours?
Social skills
Introvert/Extrovert?

If your life is not going where you envisioned, you feel you should be doing more/less, or you are not sure how to get started/back on track, we need to talk and discover what is hindering you from being all you were designed to be.
