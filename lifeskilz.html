LIFESKILZ

The definition of insanity is doing the same thing over and over again expecting a different result; it’s like a dog chasing their tail. What do they do when they catch it? Fall down, get up and start the process again! It is my assignment to show you how to stop chasing your tail and become what you were put on this earth to be and enjoy your life while doing it. Who, what, when, where, why and how are you really? How do you measure in the following areas of Life Skilz? Click here to read more!

The Five Ws, Five Ws and one H, are questions whose answers are considered basic in information-gathering. They are often mentioned in journalism, research, and police investigations. They constitute a formula for getting the complete story on a subject. According to the principle of the Five Ws, a report can only be considered complete if it answers these questions starting with an interrogative word.

Can you give a good report on yourself based on the formula referenced above on the life skilz listed below?

  • Personal care – Do you like you enough to take the very best care of you?
  • Organization – Is your life in order/disorder
  • Respect for self and others – If you don’t love and cherish yourself you will never do the same for others
  • Communication – Do you understand my verbal and nonverbal or do I send a conflicting message? Do I have the ability to understand yours?
  • Social skills – Introvert/Extrovert?

If your life is not going where you envisioned, you feel you should be doing more/less, or you are not sure how to get started/back on track, we need to talk and discover what is hindering you from being all you were designed to be.
